# Glossary

## CMP
Chromatic Management Protocol. The governance and mission-control layer that decides what agents may do.

## MCP
Model Context Protocol. The tool/data access layer for connecting models and agents to external systems.

## ADK
Agent Development Kit. A framework for building, debugging, and deploying agents.

## Magnet
A deterministic observability probe attached to an inflection point in a workflow.

## Inflection Point
A decision or transition moment where agent behavior can drift, fail, or require scoring.

## Bead
A structured action/intake item generated from a finding, alert, review, or next-step recommendation.

## Agent Lead
A synthesis role that reviews Magnet reports, produces final findings, and prepares PDR/handoff packages.

## Human Gate
A required approval point for irreversible, costly, high-risk, or low-confidence actions.
